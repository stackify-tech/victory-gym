ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE!

Victory Fitness Unisex Gym for Total Fitness is the realization of C. Ravi's lifelong passion for health and wellness. A distinguished powerlifting champion, Ravi broke two national records on his way to winning the Junior Strongman title at the State Junior Powerlifting Championship in February 1984 at just 22 years old. In 1994, he achieved another national record in the bench press category by lifting an impressive 152.5 kilograms. Established on March 18, 2018, and located in the heart of Peroorkada, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, Victory Fitness is dedicated to empowering individuals on their fitness journeys. The gym offers a variety of programs tailored to all fitness levels, ensuring everyone can achieve their personal health goals. With expert trainers, modern facilities, and a welcoming community, Victory Fitness provides classes such as strength training, Fat loss, Nutrition Consultation and more to foster total fitness and well-being. Whether you're taking your first step or you're a seasoned athlete, we are here to support you every step of the way.
