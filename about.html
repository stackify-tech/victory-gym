Frequently Asked Questions




What are your gym's operating hours?

Our gym is open from 5:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays and 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekends.

Do you offer personal training services?

Yes, we offer personal training sessions tailored to your fitness goals. Contact our front desk or visit our website to book a session.

Are there any membership plans or trial passes available?

We have flexible membership plans (monthly, quarterly, and yearly) and a one-week trial pass for new members to explore our facilities.

What facilities and equipment are available at the gym?

Our gym is equipped with a variety of cardio machines, strength training equipment, free weights, and functional training areas. We also have locker rooms, showers, and a dedicated yoga space

Is there parking available at the gym?

Yes, we provide ample parking space for our members. Bicycle racks are also available for eco-friendly commuters.
