ABOUT US

WHO ARE WE!

Victory Zumba Fitness Unisex Gym is the vision of Ravi C., a passionate fitness enthusiast who turned his dream into reality by establishing this premier fitness destination on March 18, 2018. Located in the vibrant community of Peroorkada, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, we are committed to redefining fitness and wellness in a way that is accessible to everyone. At Victory Zumba Fitness, we cater to all fitness levels and offer a variety of programs designed to help you meet your individual fitness goals. Whether you're just starting your fitness journey or you're an experienced athlete, our expert trainers and modern facilities provide the support you need to succeed. More than just a gym, we are a community where health, fitness, and motivation come together. With a wide range of classes like Zumba, strength training, and more, we create an environment that encourages personal growth, wellness, and a positive attitude. Step into Victory Zumba Fitness today and take the first step toward becoming your healthiest and happiest self. Let us help you stay active, strong, and confident every step of the way.
